📊 AtliQ Sales & Finance Analysis Report (Excel)
🚀 Project Overview

This project analyzes the sales and financial performance of AtliQ Hardware, a global FMCG company, using Microsoft Excel. The dashboard helps business leaders monitor sales, profitability, customer performance, and market trends to support data-driven decision-making.

🎯 Project Objective
Analyze sales performance across different countries and customers.
Compare Actual Sales with Target Sales.
Measure Gross Margin and Profitability.
Identify top-performing and low-performing markets.
Build an interactive Excel dashboard for business reporting.
Generate actionable insights for management.

🔍 Project Insights
✅ Market Analysis
USA generated the highest Net Sales (87.78M).
India recorded one of the highest Gross Margins (51.6M).
Japan achieved the highest Gross Margin % (46.5%).
Several markets missed their annual sales targets, highlighting opportunities for improvement.
✅ Customer Analysis
Amazon was one of the top revenue-generating customers.
Multiple customers showed strong year-over-year sales growth.
Customer performance varied significantly, indicating opportunities for targeted sales strategies.
✅ Financial Analysis
Gross Margin % differed across markets, helping identify more profitable regions.
Comparing COGS and Net Sales revealed which markets delivered stronger profitability.
Target vs Actual analysis highlighted markets needing better sales planning.

💡 Business Recommendations
Focus on high-profit markets such as Japan and India.
Improve sales strategies in markets with larger target gaps.
Strengthen relationships with top-performing customers.
Optimize pricing and product mix to improve Gross Margin.
Use dashboard insights for quarterly business reviews.
✅ Project Outcome
Built an interactive Excel dashboard for sales and financial reporting.
Simplified complex business data into easy-to-understand visual reports.
Improved decision-making by tracking KPIs across customers and markets.
Delivered business insights that support strategic sales and profitability planning.
